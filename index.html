Still... why is it better than other frameworks?

  • Simplicity - single concept of component vs controllers, directives, services, factories, providers...
  • Better reasoning - uni-directional flow vs two-way data binding
  • Performance - Virtual DOM vs Dirty-checking and static templates
  • Scalability - Additionally... it's used in production by Facebook, Instagram, Yahoo for their key products.
